Something interesting....

Huy Fong Foods cannot use the sriracha name as a registered trademark as it also happens to be a tiny fishing village in Vietnam

Mr. Tran and his business haven't spent a dollar on advertising since the company's inception - but it doesn't appear they even need it.

Since the name cannot be trademarked, other companies have been producing "sriracha"-style hot sauces, Heinz for example.

However an unintended effect of the usage of the sriracha name by other companies is that they are basically advertising for Huy Fong Foods sriracha sauce at the same time

Mr. Tran's scrumptious sauce has already conquered the globe - by word of mouth alone.
